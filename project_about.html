Our Story

Founded in the heart of Hickory, NC, Jacob’s Guitars began as a dream to bring high-quality instruments to local musicians. We specialize in boutique electric and acoustic guitars, amplifiers, and gear you won’t find anywhere else.

Whether you’re a beginner buying your first acoustic or a professional musician looking for your next custom built electric guitar, our goal is to help you find the perfect gear for your sound.

Stop by our store or browse our online shop to experience Hickory’s most unique collection of guitars!
